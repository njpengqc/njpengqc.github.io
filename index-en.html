Speech

Hello everyone, the theme of my speech today is "Insects", the book says: "When scarabs choose their own food, they first use the serrated top shell to dig a few times, and then do a search to remove impurities. The scarab uses its front legs to gather the food between the four legs under the abdomen, and the four curved hind legs press lightly, the food becomes a spherical shape, and the food is gently pressed by the four feet while rocking." The scarab beetle in Made their own food so carefully, made into a ball to make it easier for them to roll, but when they worked so hard to do it, an uninvited guest snatched it away as if its an effort A joke to him, as did the author of "Insects", when Fabre was 3 years old, he was fostered at his grandparents' house, sleeping with sheep in the winter, and at 6 years old, in order to increase the family income, he helped to look after the ducklings , left home alone at the age of 14 to sell lemons and work as a railway worker to support himself, not only in his youth but also in his later years. At the age of 46, he borrowed money from Miller. In order to support a family of seven, the burden was very heavy. At the age of 77, because of imitations on the market, the royalties were reduced, and his life was in trouble. Don't you want to be like the scarab, he worked very hard, and the fact gave him so much sadness, and in such a predicament, at the age of 86 At the time, Fabre completed the creation of the eleventh chapter of "Insects".

And we are sitting in a bright and bright classroom with a worry-free learning mood and a generous and thick textbook. What reason do we have for not studying hard? In the movie "Changjin Lake", the soldiers dressed in single clothes and cloth shoes fought bravely in the cold environment of more than 30 degrees below zero. There were planes on board for reconnaissance, and enemy troops were stationed in front of them. They rested in the cold and ate raw potatoes. , Some people even gnawed their teeth, but in the end, the Volunteer Army not only defeated the "United Nations Army" led by MacArthur who underestimated the enemy, routed 400 kilometers from the Yalu River and stabilized the front near the 38th parallel. It also gave the United States its first defeat since the founding of the nation. If the Volunteers did not have steel will and optimism, how could they have won this battle? If Fabre did not have strong will and optimism, how could he have completed the masterpiece "Insects" at such a time of embarrassment? So if we don't have the spirit of not giving up and optimism, how can we get good grades in school? So shouldn't we study hard and strive to be the pillars of the country?

This concludes my speech, thank you all for listening, thank you!
